Cornell ChemE Car

Engineering | Problem Solving | Team Collaboration

Cornell University ChemE Car is a project team in which students of all majors work together to build several model cars purely powered and stopped by chemical reactions. After months of design, construction and careful calibrations, we compete each spring at the AlChE Northeast Regional Conference.
